AI Guided Product Design and Optimalization

Behavior and output performance and qualitative parameters of complex products cen be predicted in the design phase.E.g.: from their geometrical parameters captured in CAD models.

Such approach allows:

  • Avoid creating physical prototypes and CFD models

  • Uncover unknown dependencies that can be applied by engineers and are unknown to them

  • Make recommendation on how to modify product/machine parameters to influence its behavior

Vision & Values

With an incredibly fast-paced technology development, we are standing right now at the fateful junction. Technology can help us solve most of the problems we face as humanity. But they also have the potential to destroy us. Stopping their development is not real and sitting and waiting for it to happen, we do not own.

our vision

We believe that Ethics will not hinder us or our clients. On the contrary, it will be a great competitive advantage and the positive impact of the things we work on will allow us to bring together the best experts. We can make sense of their passion and passion in the right direction - leaving the planet a better place for our children.

our values

We simply go to projects that we feel good about and believe they can improve the world. We also choose who we work with and for whom we work.

research reports

In addition to our own research projects, we closely co-operate with top-class universities and support applied research. Collaboration and sharing know-how is our next key asset. That is why we teach, lecture, publish and contribute actively to the communities where we are members.

lastest research

We track the latest technologies, explore them, and have a very good idea of ​​their possible benefits. In our view, we are positive and we perceive that it will be more about the strengths of both worlds than about their duels. This synergy can move us further as humanity and ensure that we do not destroy and leave the world a better place for our children and for the next generation.
