AI Guided Product Design and Optimalization

Behavior and output performance and qualitative parameters of complex products cen be predicted in the design phase.E.g.: from their geometrical parameters captured in CAD models.

Such approach allows:

  • Avoid creating physical prototypes and CFD models

  • Uncover unknown dependencies that can be applied by engineers and are unknown to them

  • Make recommendation on how to modify product/machine parameters to influence its behavior

Digital Twin

Digital Twin is a software component that mimics behavior of a physical system or a product. Unlike physical systems that are closed, have hidden states, give noisy and delayed information. The digital twin is open, 100% transparent to the users, gives actual infromation with supressed noise. Actual and future states are estimated from a sequence of historical and actual observations of the original physical system.

Digital twin can make predictions

Digital twin can predict future development of machine states and therefore allows acting in advance.

Digital twin is active not passive

Digital twin generates alerts and events as a reaction on important change, anomaly, state reached.

Digital twin is partition tolerant

Digital twin can provide machine states before they are actually delivered from the machine or even when the real machine is disconnected for some time.

Digital twin can analyze root causes and influences

Digital twin stores historical development of its states as well as interactions with other systems - therefore can back correlate what influenced.

360º view of the system

The quality largerly depends on the percentace of influencing information on its disposal. Putting a physical system into its full context gives much bigger plausability and accuracy of outputs.
